# React-PDF + AI: The Builder's Guide to Premium PDF Generation

**Practical patterns for developers who ship.**

*By Landon Miles*

---

You have an AI coding agent, a React codebase, and a deadline. You need to generate PDFs that don't look like they were made by a robot. This book gives you the exact project structure, design token system, and prompting patterns to make `@react-pdf/renderer` and AI tools produce output your clients will actually pay for.
